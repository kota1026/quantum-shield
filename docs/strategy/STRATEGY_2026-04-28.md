## 6. How this connects to the 11-agent E2E orchestrator

The orchestrator landed in `src/agents/e2e-orchestrator/` is the verification
spine for the audit-first decision in §4(C). It is the autonomous pre-Sherlock
gate: every PR that touches `src/api/api/`, `src/frontend/web/`, or
`src/contracts/` runs through `qs-e2e --sequence <name>` in CI. Verdicts other
than `PASS` block merge. Sherlock auditors will see a clean `last-green`
record per sequence rather than ad-hoc claims.

Concretely, the orchestrator runs are part of the Sherlock submission
package: `reports/.last-green/lock.json`, `reports/.last-green/unlock.json`,
`reports/.last-green/slashing.json` are committed as artifacts the contest
judges can replay.
