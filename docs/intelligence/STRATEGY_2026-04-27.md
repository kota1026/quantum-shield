|---|---|---|

| Generic ECDSA wallets | None | Quantum-vulnerable |
| **Quantum Shield** | **ML-DSA + SPHINCS+ dual NIST on EVM** | — |

**Hedge**: dual algorithms from independent mathematical families (lattice + hash). If either is broken, the other still protects funds.
